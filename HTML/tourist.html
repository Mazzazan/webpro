Tour Saudi Arabia

For people interested in having a tour around Saudi Arabia, I suggest the Ithra located my hometown Al-Dammam. Though I haven't went there myself, my parents and siblings did while I was here in Indonesia, I could tell they had a great time from the pictures they sent to me. I wasn't brought out a lot back then but would go to malls every Friday and would go to an area near the shore known as "Cornniche" beside my homoetown. Not only is it very accessible, many locals prefer going to corniche to spend their time with family. While the children run around and play in the field or theh provided playground.
