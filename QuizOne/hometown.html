Hometown

I was born at Jeddah in Saudi Arabia, and lived there for quite a while I then remember moving to Dammam when I was reaching close to 5 years of age and ever since, I had been living there. The climate there is extremely hot, reaching up to 50 degrees on an extremely hot day. Altough majority of the people think Saudi Arabia as a full desert. Contrary to what many think, Saudi Arabia is a very well-built country with strong economy and great infrastructure. The citizens being especially generous and understanding, you can expect to make friends with the locals and might end up to their home for a hearty meal together filled with joyous laughters and a fulfilling night.
